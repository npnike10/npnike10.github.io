Niket Parikh

I am a PhD student at the Lab for Intelligent Robots and Agents (LIRA), part of the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign , where I work on multi-agent reinforcement learning. My PhD advisor is Prof. Huy T. Tran. Previously, I graduated from IIT Bombay with a Dual degree (Bachelor of Technology + Master of Technology) in Aerospace Engineering.

Research

I'm interested in reinforcement learning, deep learning, and game theory. My current research focuses on multi-agent reinforcement learning, with an emphasis on understanding and enhancing the social outcome from the interaction of self-interested agents.
